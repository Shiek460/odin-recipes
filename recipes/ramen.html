Ramen Nodle Soup

Description

This soup is just very, very good... Find ramen noodles at most supermarkets, or Asian grocery stores.

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1(3.5 oz) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp chili oil
  • 1/2 tsp minced ginger root
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine broth and noodles.
  2. Cover and bring to boil; stir to break up noodles
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil, and ginger
  4. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes
  5. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions
